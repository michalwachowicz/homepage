About Me

Hey there! I'm SpongeBob SquarePants, a fun-loving sponge living in Bikini Bottom. I work as a fry cook at the Krusty Krab and love spending time with my best friend Patrick, jellyfishing, and practicing karate with Sandy.

My Friends

Patrick Star

Patrick is my best friend in the whole ocean! He might not be the smartest, but he's always there for me. We love jellyfishing, going on crazy adventures, or just doing nothing together. No one can make me laugh like Patrick!

Sandy Cheeks

Sandy is my super-smart, karate-loving friend from Texas! She’s a squirrel who lives in a treedome and can do just about anything. We have so much fun practicing karate and going on wild adventures. She’s the toughest and smartest in Bikini Bottom!

Squidward Tentacles

Squidward is my neighbor and co-worker at the Krusty Krab. He’s a talented artist and musician, but he can be a bit grumpy and likes his peace and quiet. I always try to befriend him, even if he doesn’t appreciate my enthusiasm. Deep down, I know he has a good heart!

Mr. Krabs

Mr. Krabs is my boss at the Krusty Krab and a big fan of money! He’s tough and loves running his restaurant, but he really cares about his employees (especially me!). He’s always looking for ways to make a buck, but I know he has a soft spot for his daughter, Pearl, and his Krabby Patties!j

Larry the Lobster

Larry is the strongest lobster in Bikini Bottom and the king of the Goo Lagoon! He’s all about fitness and loves showing off his muscles. I admire his confidence and enthusiasm for life. Whenever I need motivation to have fun in the sun, I look to Larry for inspiration!

Mrs. Puff

Mrs. Puff is my boating school teacher, and she’s the best! She’s really patient with me, even when I accidentally crash her boat (again). She cares about her students and wants us to succeed, but I know she gets a little stressed out sometimes. I just want to make her proud and finally pass my boating test!
